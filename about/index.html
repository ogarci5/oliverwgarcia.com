About Me

Howdy! I'm Oliver Garcia. The best way to describe me is that I am a Jack of all trades, master of none. I enjoy pretty much anything, whether it involves solving a complex physics problem or learning how to play a new song on guitar.
In high school, I was known to carry a guitar around whilst fixing peoples' computers when they caught viruses all while competing in robotics competitions.
